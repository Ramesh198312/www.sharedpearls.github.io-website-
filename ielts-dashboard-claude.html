IELTS 9-Band Scale

9
Expert user
Full operational command; accurate, fluent and complete understanding.
8
Very good user
Fully operational command with only occasional inaccuracies.
7
Good user
Handles complex language well, with occasional errors or misunderstanding.
6
Competent user
Generally effective command, especially in familiar situations.
5
Modest user
Partial command; can cope with overall meaning but makes many mistakes.

Band 7+ Examiner Tips

Concise reminders based on official IELTS assessment expectations and frequent candidate errors.

Listening

Critical rule

Do not write the first attractive answer immediately. Wait for corrections and final decisions.

Reading

Critical rule

False means the text contradicts the statement. Not Given means the information is absent.

Writing

Critical rule

Task 1 needs an overview. Task 2 needs a clear position and developed support.

Speaking

Critical rule

Avoid memorised scripts. Practise flexible frameworks and natural examples instead.
